Who am I?

I am an interaction designer in the making.

Hi there! My name is Mahit.

I am a final year student at the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati, pursuing a bachelor of design course and specialising in user experience design and human-computer interaction. (Phew, that's a moutful!).

I believe a designer can choose to either design to make lives easier, or make lives better. Design for me is more than just a pretty picture or a fancy prototype. What matters is how much of a difference you can make in someone's life, no matter how small the problem. At the end of the day, its the little things that matter!

Beyond design, I enjoy lawn tennis and hit the courts often. I'm also a huge sit-com person and they keep me on the saner side everytime things get too difficult to handle. Oh, I write too!

You can find me on LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram and Facebook. If not, you can always drop me an email at sreemahit@gmail.com. I'm always up for a nice conversation about absolutely anything!

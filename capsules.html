Capsules: News Has
Never Been Better

Role User Research
and Interaction Design
Team Solo Project
Duration 12 Weeks,
Fall 2019

Process Deck



The Problem

I noticed a considerable dip in the amount of news consumed by my college mates and other students in this age group. Knowing the importance of keeping abreast of what's happening around the world to atleast a certain extent, I set out to fix this problem.


The Solution

Capsules is a modern-day news aggregator that personalises news updates, by delivering a curated number of relevant and appropriate articles depending on the reader's convenience, giving the reader granuate control over her news consumption.


Domain Understanding

To begin with, an intuitive understanding of news and how its perceived was noted down from the researcher’s point of view. Since the researcher also fell into the category of users targetted, all views about this subject were put down.

Types of Users

1. Don't keep up to date and don't want to.
2. Understand the need to, but don't keep up to date.
3. Occasionally keep up to date.
4. Regularly keep up to date, with and without fixed sources.
5. Voraciously keep up to date.

Target Demographic

There is a decrease in awareness about news and its importance primarily among college students and young working professionals. Reasons could include a change in lifestyle, lack of awareness and no insistance upon this matter.

Primary Research

The objective of the research was to learn why there is a dearth in acquiring news among the target audience in the second and third category audience. One-on-one interview sessions were conducted with a total of three college students and two working professionals in Bangalore City. Beyond this, a form was floated and 15 responses from all across India were recorded.


Top Findings

I analysed all the responses and grouped them according to recurring themes and patterns. These groups were reframed to obtain six areas of attention and interest.




Opportunity Areas

From the top findings and insights I gathered from the research, I narrowed down on two major opportunity areas I wanted to focus on.

Opportunity Area 1

“How Might We reduce the time taken to gather critical news updates?”


Opportunity Area 2

“How Might We intice users into voluntarily catching up with the latest news updates?”


Goals

With these directions, I arrived upon four important goals that I aimed to achieve with my solution.


Design Direction

A traditional newspaper satisfies all the goals stated above but isn't appealing to a crowd of young adults and teenagers who are constantly found on their phones.

I propose a smart way of providing the same advantages of a newspaper to the tech-savvy milennials of today.

It will act as a news aggregrator, sourcing news from reputable and trust worthy sources of the news industry, while also having minor authenticity checks by itself. It will curate and short list only the most relevant news articles for each consumer, using certain smart tags alloted to each article. This will ensure only the most relevant updates are shown to the consumer. It will restrict the number of articles for each session to enable acquiring news updates within 5 minutes flat, or another time frame depending on the users’ wishes. Breaking away from a continuous scroll option, this approach was chosen keeping in mind the user‘s academic/corporat priorities. It will also provide crisp and clear titles for each article, to ensure the meaning is understood without going through the entire article. If need be, summaries of various lengths will be available.




Learning

This project was quite the learning experience. Being the very first project I took up all by myself, it gave me an insight into all the areas of the design process and taught me how to handle every step appropriately. There are several places which need to be improved and I hope to get back to working on this soon.


Scope

The design phase of this project will include planning the information architecture of the application, wireframing and designing high fidelity mock ups. The project documented so far is the user research that has gone into the understanding the thought that goes into acquiring news. With a concrete design direction in place, the design phase can begin.

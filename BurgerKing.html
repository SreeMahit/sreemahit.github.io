Grill The Nation -
Advertising Campaign

Role Ideation and
Visual Design
Team Anshuman Dhar,
Niranjan Bharati
Duration 1 Week

The Problem

The brief was given by Burger King through the D&AD New Blood Awards 2018. THe challenge was to come up with an advertising campaign that would help the newer generations connect with Burger King and their message, 'Flame Grilling is Better'.


The Solution

We realised that the target audience of 18-25 year olds love using platforms where their voice can be heard. #GrilTheNation taps into everyone who wishes to raise their voice against problems they are facing which can be made better by the government.






Learning

This challenge was a lot of fun as we got to go through several ad campaigns Burger King has undertaken, to understand their marketing style and scope.


Scope

With a litte more time, we would have spent time piloting the campaign to see how effective it might be.

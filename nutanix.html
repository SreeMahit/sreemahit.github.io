Summer Internship
at Nutanix, India

Role User Experience Design
and Interaction Design

Mentors Susheel Kewaley,
Amarkant Singh

Duration 2 Months,
Summer 2019

The Problem

I worked with the Xi Beam team and worked on the Beam Security Compliance product. I was tasked with redesigning one component of the security compliance vertical of Xi Beam, which slowly ballooned into a complete overhaul of the component itself, and the product to a certain extent.


The Solution

The internship involves a thorough redesign of the experience of the product, which culminated in medium-fidelity mockups of dashboards for the product.




The Process

The project included several cycles of intense brainstorming sessions with the product manager (PM), quick research into the problem statement, paper sketches of concepts and medium-fidelity wireframe iterations culminating in a discussion with the PM as to whether the proposed iteration aligns with where the product is headed.

After several weeks of diverging into the problem space, I began converging onto the finer details and incorporating the Nutanix Design guidelines towards the last few weeks, just before the internship ended. The final set of deliverables included high fidelity mock-ups for Xi Beam which, after a quick visual design review, can be sent over for development.


Owing to nondisclosure agreements with Nutanix, I cannot divulge further information unfortunately. Do drop me an email at sreemahit@gmail.com for further details about the project.



Learning

This internship provided me with the opportunity to spearhead my own project from start to finish, which included coordinating with the various stakeholders of the product. Coupled with handling the industry deadlines, the internship proved to be an invaluable learning experience.

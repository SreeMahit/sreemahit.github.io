The Club

Anchorenza and RadioG is the anchoring and content creation club of IIT Guwahati. The club covers four major domains: anchoring, interviewing, video sketches and radiojockeying. The club has an active member base of more than 30 students.

My Role

I have been associated with the club for more than 3 years, where I anchored for events, interviewed celebrities and headed the club as its secretary. For video content, I have donned the hat of director, script writing and anchor for various sketches.


More than just cricket | A Conversation with Brett Lee

I had the wonderful opportunity to interview Brett Lee, a former International cricketer in the Australian Cricket Team. I was incharge of research, scripting and conducting the interview itself.





Mr. & Ms. Fresher 2018 - Promo

Anchorenza and RadioG organises the annual Mr. & Ms. Fresher event, where freshmen compete in a series of fun and challenging rounds to win the coveted title of Mr. Fresher or Ms. Fresher. I was incharge of the promotional video for the 2018 edition, where I, along with Samadrita Ghosh, scripted, directed, shot and edited the video.





The man behind Techniche '18: A chat with Purvish Shah

We interviewed Purvish Shah, the convener of Techniche 2018, the annual techno-manegement festival of IIT Guwahati. I was incharge of overseeing the shoot and edit.





The people behind Alcheringa '19: A chat with Ayush and Tharun

We interviewed Ayush Shivali and Tharun Patnaik, the overall coordinators of Alcheringa 2019, the annual cultural festival of IIT Guwahati. I was incharge of overseeing the shoot.




Apart from these, I have actively been involved in several other videos in a limited role, both behind and in front of the camera, all of which can be found on the Anchorenza and RadioG YouTube channel.
